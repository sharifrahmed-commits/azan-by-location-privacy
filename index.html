Privacy Policy - Azan by Location

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Azan by Location helps users view prayer times and listen to Azan based on location. No account is required.

Information We Use

  • Location (precise or approximate): Used to calculate prayer times and display local city information.
  • Notifications: Used to send prayer-time alerts when enabled by the user.

How Data Is Stored

The app stores limited data locally on your device, including selected settings, last known coordinates, and recently fetched prayer times for offline display.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. The app may call third-party APIs (for prayer times, geocoding, or weather) to provide core functionality.

Your Choices

  • You can deny location permission, but prayer-time accuracy/features may be limited.
  • You can disable notifications at any time in app settings or device settings.
  • You can use manual location selection instead of device location.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
